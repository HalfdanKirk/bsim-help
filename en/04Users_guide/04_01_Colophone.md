# Colophone
**Danish Building Research Institute** [publications](https://www.build.aau.dk/til-byggebranchen) are issued in the following series: Directions, Results, Documentations and Concrete. The publications can be obtained through book shops or by taking out an subscription. The Institute's annual report, catalogue of publications are available free of charge from Danish Building Research Institute.

**The Subscription** is a discount scheme offering many advantages to people wishing to be kept up to date on specific subjects within the building research field. Call Danish Building Research Institute for further information.
